Birthday Parties

Do you want your birthday to jump-off-the-walls? Book with us today for unforgettable, and no stress, party! Our birthday package includes full access to all trampolines, foam pits, courses, and attractions. We reserve a private room for you and all of your friends so you can enjoy, concessions, outside catering, cake, or any other treats or gifts. A private party host will be around the entire time to keep the fun going and help with any issues. We will handle all planning, set up, and clean up so you can enjoy your day stress free! School Field Trip

Plan the ultimate school field trip! Did your students earn an end of the year party? Join us for unforgetable fun and team building activities. From challenging obstacle courses to dodgeball games, the kids will never want to leave. Next the students will be able to free jump until lunchtime that we will fully cater for you! After eating we will lead them in team building games, and watch as they cheer on their peers in the obstacle course. Book your trip today!

  • Price per student/jumper: $35.00

Pricing includes general addmission ticket and access to the ninja course. This also accounts for the reserved party room and concessions.
